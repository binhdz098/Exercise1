About Hamburger

A hamburger is a popular global dish, originating from the USA. It consists of a grilled or fried ground beef patty placed between two bread slices. It’s often served with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, and various sauces. Cheese, bacon, or eggs may be added for extra flavor. It’s a staple of street food culture worldwide.
